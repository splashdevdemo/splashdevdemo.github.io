Flex tariff

Do you want to reap the benefits of price fluctuations in the energy market? Flex tariff is the plan for you. The rates for kWh and therms are based on the Electricity Price Index and are calculated monthly. We can send you pricing information via e-mail or SMS.

What do you get with this tariff?

Consumer price Calculated monthly.
Energy type Nuclear, Coal, Fuel Oil, Waste incineration, Geothermal, Hydropower
Bills Online, Per post
Minimum contract term 24 months
Contract cancellation fee 10 Euro

Why should you choose this tariff?

  • You benefit from changes in prices on the electricity market immediately.
  • The monthly changes in prices are presented to you in a clear and understandable way.
  • You are secure thanks to a price cap: in the first 12 months, the consumption price can’t exceed the price cap.
Prepayment tariff

If you are in town for a short period of time or if you know your energy needs well, the prepayment tariff is a good choice for you. You set a target and pay for it in advance. There is also no minimum contract duration in the prepayment tariff plan.

What do you get with this tariff?

Consumer price 0,07 per kWh for kWh within target
0,14 per therm for therm within target
Standard rates for extra consumption
Energy type Nuclear, Coal, Fuel Oil, Waste incineration, Geothermal, Hydropower
Bills Online
Minimum contract term No minimum contract term
Contract cancellation fee None

Why should you choose this tariff?

  • You get lower fixed prices per kWh and per therm.
  • You don’t want to be tied down by a contract.
  • If your consumption exceeds your prepaid amount, you can top it up with a new online payment. Standard rates apply for the extra kWh and the extra therms.
