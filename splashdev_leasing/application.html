Conditions of termination (cancellation)

You may, at any time after six months from the date of this Lease, cancel this Lease by seven days prior written notice to SL. The Lease will cease at the end of the notice period (‘date of cancellation’). As a condition of cancellation, the Goods must be delivered to the Dealer (unless you are otherwise directed by SL) on, or prior to, the date of cancellation. If you fail to deliver the Goods, this Lease will continue in force. On, or prior to, the date of cancellation, you must pay SL:

  • any payment and any other amount then due and payable under this Lease but unpaid;
  • the amount (if any) calculated as at the date of cancellation by which the book value of the Goods (which is to include the relevant portion of each fee, charge and insurance premium specified under the heading 'Lease details' in this Disclosure Statement exceeds the then current wholesale market value of the Goods, both as certified by, or on behalf of, SL;
  • an amount to compensate SL for its loss resulting from early cancellation (and, in particular, if any fixed rate funding arrangement entered into in processing your finance application must be broken) calculated as at the date of cancellation in accordance with the formula set out in clause 6 of Section 1 of the Standard Terms;
  • $50.00 SL administration costs (including any GST).
Notices
